How To Make A Website

With Adobe XD

Step 1: Start By Installing Adobe XD

You can do this by going to their site on: 
https://www.adobe.com/products/xd.html. And just
click the download button or the Get Adobe Xd button.
And go through the installation process.

Step 2: Start By Creating Your Own Document

You can choose what kind of document you
want to build by choosing one of the options
Adobe Xd gives. But for this project you will
want to choose Custom. Then you will want
to create an artboard with the art board tool.
